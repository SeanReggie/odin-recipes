Pork Pozole with All the Fixins'

Pork pozole is simple to make and requires a good handful of essential ingredients such as:

  • Pork
  • Hominy
  • Broth
  • Onion
  • Several types of dried peppers
  • Cilantro
  • Cabbage
  • Radish
  • Plenty of salt
A good squeeze of lime never killed anyone either!

Steps:

  1. Sear pork pieces
  2. Add broth
  3. Cook hominy in separate pot
  4. Deseed dried peppers and boil them in water for 10 minutes until soft
  5. Add softened peppers and pork broth to blender and blend
  6. Yada, yada, yada, add everythnig in big pot and cook until done
